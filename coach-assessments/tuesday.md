# Situation

Hartwell manages 4000 high net worth clients across the UK with a good compliance record, and the firm recently completed a transition from a heavily customised legacy system to a cloud-based system.

## Complication

A review showed that 47/200 sampled records were non-complient, because the transition transferred documents that were not fully populated with mandatory data. Internal analysis showed that 1100 records are missing data with half of these requiring significant touch-ups, and the firm has a strict 90-day window to present a credible remidiation plan to the FCA.

## Question

How can Hartwell's remidiation plan satisfy the FCA's regulatory policies?

## Answer

Present the FCA with a clear project roadmap that does not frame the issue as a total loss of control. Add lots and lots of guardrails to your solution that closely follow the regulations from the FCA.
